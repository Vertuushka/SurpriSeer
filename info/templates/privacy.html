Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SurpriSeer ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit and use our website.

Information We Collect

When you register on our website, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: Your username or full name provided by Google.
  • Email Address: The email address linked to your Google account.
  • Registration Date: The date you registered on our website.
  • Last Login Date: The date of your most recent login to the site.

This information is automatically collected from your Google account during registration and authentication.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Identify Users: Your name and email address help us identify and authenticate you on the website.
  • Communication: We may use your email address to communicate with you, including support inquiries or sending important notifications, such as changes to this Privacy Policy or other essential updates.

How We Protect Your Information

We use standard security practices provided by the Django framework to protect your data.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties. Only the website owner has access to your data, and it is not disclosed or transferred to external parties.

Cookies

Our website uses standard cookies provided by the Django framework to improve the user experience. These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the website and are not used for tracking or advertising purposes.

Managing Your Data

Since the information we collect is directly retrieved from your Google account, we do not manually edit or update this data. You can manage and update your profile information directly through your account settings.

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can submit a request to our support. Once your account is deleted, all related data will be permanently removed from our database, and you will receive a notification via email confirming the deletion. This will be the last time your email address is used by us, and it will be removed from our records.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for users under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete such information as soon as possible.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated via email to users, and the updated version will be posted on this page.

Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time. To do so, please contact our support. Once your request is processed, all data associated with your account will be deleted.
